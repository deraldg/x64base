14:T44c,<p>x64base® is the synthesis of three threads that started in 1993:</p>
<ol>
<li><strong>Practical engineering</strong> — building stable data tools that keep working year after year.</li>
<li><strong>Teaching</strong> — treating file formats and relational thinking as learnable, inspectable systems.</li>
<li><strong>Historical continuity</strong> — respecting the xBase lineage while modernizing it for 64-bit systems.</li>
</ol>
<p>From the beginning, the aim was <em>transparent computing</em>: you can open the files, understand the structures,
and reason about performance and correctness without magic.</p>
<p>By 2026, that mindset becomes a complete ecosystem:</p>
<ul>
<li>A canonical 64-bit engine (DBF_64 + FPT64)</li>
<li>A learning-first scripting language (DotTalk++)</li>
<li>Focused CLI and front-end tools (TupTalk / TableTalk / RelTalk / ArcticTalk)</li>
<li>A free non-profit / learning edition (LabTalk)</li>
</ul>
