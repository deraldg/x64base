15:Tcbb,<h2 id="primary-author"><a class="no-underline" href="#primary-author">Primary Author</a></h2>
<p><strong>Derald R Grimwood Jr.</strong> is the originator and primary author of the x64base, DotTalk++, DotScript, and Laboratory Campus / LabTalk concept line. His background includes SAP R/3 ABAP/4, HR/Payroll and Finance systems, database conversion, payroll and business software, U.S. Army finance service, and part-time computer instruction at Lane Community College. The project preserves the xBase lineage while using modern C++ implementation work, self-documenting contracts, proof-labeled documentation, and an education-first laboratory model.</p>
<p>See <a href="/docs/dev/developer-profile">Developer Profile</a> for the project-relevant professional background behind the current development work.</p>

Human review remains the authority for release decisions, authorship, licensing, and trademark use.</p>
