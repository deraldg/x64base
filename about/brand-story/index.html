Brand Story

Technical. Educational. Historical. Modern.

x64base balances two goals that are often separated:

  • Production stability — predictable, durable behavior.
  • Database literacy — teaching how tables, memos, and indexes really work.

The tone is codex-style clarity:

  • short sentences
  • explicit definitions
  • stable terminology
  • minimal marketing fluff

This brand story supports a documentation-first platform — like Rust, PostgreSQL, Python, and SQLite — while being uniquely rooted in the 1993–2026 lineage.

Voice guidelines

  • Prefer precise words over hype.
  • Explain the why before the how.
  • Show file format details and examples.
  • Keep concepts composable.
