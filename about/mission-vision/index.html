Mission & Vision

Why x64base exists and where it’s going.

Mission

To democratize database literacy by providing clear, stable, transparent tools that teach as much as they perform.

Vision

A world where students, developers, and communities understand data structures, file formats, and relational thinking through approachable, historically grounded tools.

x64base

A true 64-bit evolution of the xBase lineage. Born in 1993. Reimagined for 2026.
