The full-stack documentation vertical has completed all nine controlled gates,
from comments, contracts, HELP, and manualgen through public-source staging,
website publication, and cache-bypassed live verification.

The accepted checkpoint includes:

- a 4,118-line, 237-heading developer manual;
- 183 command-reference pages: 164 reader-linked and 19 supplemental;
- 3,974 command-lineage rows;
- an 11-path reviewed website projection; and
- exact manual and publication metadata for repeatable verification.

The follow-up projection is intentionally separate. Pinocchio now has an
eight-row historical before/after benchmark ledger and a machine identity rule,
preserved with its plan, runners, validator, and closeouts in
[public source commit `853937f1`](https://github.com/deraldg/x64base/tree/853937f1f612c1a41a8fbda2c8d99e55fe44b9a3).
The original runs did not record the machine, and the post-correction raw timing
transcript was not located, so the website preserves those limits instead of
turning the numbers into a release guarantee.

Documentation closeout records are now public-source preserved. Comments
collection audit records remain in their development lane pending separate
review. `METACOLLECT-238` stays a separate 238-finding mission rather than being
silently folded into the completed run.
