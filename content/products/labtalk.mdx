## Overview

LabTalk is the education and lab-facing framing for DotTalk++ / x64base.

The local project supports this direction through:

- visible table, record, field, index, relation, and workspace concepts.
- command-centered learning through DotTalk++.
- TUI, wxWidgets, and Python/Tk GUI lanes for command discovery and teaching.
- docs and manuals focused on database literacy.
- sample and smoke workflows such as MCC-style relation browsing and metadata workspace exercises.

LabTalk should be described as an educational/lab program direction, not as a finished licensed distribution unless that packaging is explicitly created.
