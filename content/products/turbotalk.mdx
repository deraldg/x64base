## Overview

TurboTalk is the automation and maintenance-facing label for repeatable DotTalk++ workflows.

In the local source tree, the real surfaces include:

- DotScript scripts and smoke/canary workflows.
- `BUILDLMDB`, `REINDEX`, and index lifecycle work.
- workspace open/save/load flows.
- `CMDHELPCHK` validation.
- maintenance scripts under `dottalkpp/scripts` and related generated proposal packages.

Use this name for workflow automation and maintenance concepts, not as a claim that a separate complete product exists.

See [TurboTalk docs](/docs/talk-family/turbotalk) and [Current Project Truth](/docs/dev/project-truth).
