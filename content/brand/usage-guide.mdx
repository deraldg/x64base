## Naming

- Use **x64base®** for the platform name.
- Use **DBF_64** and **FPT64** exactly as written.
- Use product names consistently (DotTalk++, TupTalk, TableTalk, RelTalk, TurboTalk, LabTalk).

## Tone

- technical
- educational
- historically grounded
- modern and minimal

## Style

- short sentences
- define terms once
- prefer examples over rhetoric
