## Naming

- Use **x64base®** for the platform name.
- Use **DBF_64** and **FPT64** exactly as written.
- Use product names consistently (DotTalk++, TupTalk, TableTalk, RelTalk, ArcticTalk, LabTalk).
- Use **ArcticTalk** as the public front-end umbrella brand.
- Keep **Foxtalk** and related fox-derived names for lineage/subsystem references under the Arctic umbrella.
- Preserve classic FoxPro-style syntax/history where true, but do not describe DotTalk++ as a clone.

## Tone

- technical
- educational
- historically grounded
- modern and minimal

## Style

- short sentences
- define terms once
- prefer examples over rhetoric
