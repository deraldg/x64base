## Self-registering commands (open architecture)

DotTalk++ is an open-architecture database. Most commands are registered in the
central registry (`src/cli/shell_commands.cpp`), but the engine also lets
extensions and students **self-register** their own commands without editing the
core — the same mechanism as the self-registering expression functions on the
[function catalog](/docs/dottalk/function-catalog). `STUDENTECHO` and
`STUDENTHELLO` (in `src/ext/cmd/`) are the shipped templates: copy one to add
your own command. The related `EXITS` command inspects the reviewed
extension-exit manifest that governs these additions. Self-registered commands
are recorded in the runtime `SYSCMD` catalog even though they are not in the
central registry, so they are listed here too.
