<p>RelTalk names the relation-facing workflow in the DotTalk++ / x64base system.</p>
<p>Its subject is connected data across work areas: declared relation edges,
asymmetric same-field/cross-owner links, saved workspace relation state, and
rooted traversal through <code>ERSATZ</code>.</p>
<p>The local project includes same-field relation support and asymmetric relation forms such as:</p>
<pre><code class="language-text">REL ADD SYSCMD SYSSUBCMD ON CAN_NAME TO PARENT
REL ADD SYSSUBCMD SYSENTVAR ON QUAL_NAME TO HELP_OWNR
</code></pre>
<p>Current relation-facing surfaces include:</p>
<ul>
<li><code>REL ADD</code></li>
<li><code>REL LIST ALL</code></li>
<li><code>REL ENUM</code></li>
<li><code>REL REFRESH</code></li>
<li><code>WORKSPACE SAVE</code> / <code>WORKSPACE LOAD</code></li>
<li><code>ERSATZ</code>, <code>ERSATZ PATH</code>, and <code>RBROWSE</code></li>
<li>workspace graph and GUI contract docs</li>
</ul>
