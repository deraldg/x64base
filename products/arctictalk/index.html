ArcticTalk

Public umbrella for GUI, TUI, shell-bridge, and repeatable maintenance/workbench workflows.

Overview

ArcticTalk is the public umbrella for the DotTalk++ front-end family.

In current project use, it covers the public-facing workbench/story around GUI, TUI, command-lane, and repeatable workflow surfaces. Fox-derived subsystem names such as Foxtalk remain valid inside that umbrella.

Its subject is the front-end/workbench layer around the runtime, not a claim that every tool under the umbrella is already a finished product.

In the local source tree, the real surfaces include:

  • ArcticTalk/Foxtalk TUI and shell-bridge work.
  • DotScript scripts and smoke/canary workflows.
  • BUILDLMDB, REINDEX, and index lifecycle work.
  • workspace open/save/load flows.
  • CMDHELPCHK validation.
  • maintenance scripts under dottalkpp/scripts and related generated proposal packages.

Use this name for the public front-end/workbench family and its repeatable workflow surfaces, not as a claim that a separate complete product exists.

See ArcticTalk docs, Current Project Truth, and SelfDoc Website Publication, plus the canonical public repo deraldg/x64base.
