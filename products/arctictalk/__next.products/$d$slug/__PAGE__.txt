3:T5bc,<h2 id="overview"><a class="no-underline" href="#overview">Overview</a></h2>
<p>ArcticTalk is the public umbrella for the DotTalk++ front-end family.</p>
<p>In current project use, it covers the public-facing workbench/story around GUI,
TUI, command-lane, and repeatable workflow surfaces. Fox-derived subsystem names
such as <code>Foxtalk</code> remain valid inside that umbrella.</p>
<p>Its subject is the front-end/workbench layer around the runtime, not a claim
that every tool under the umbrella is already a finished product.</p>
<p>In the local source tree, the real surfaces include:</p>
<ul>
<li>ArcticTalk/Foxtalk TUI and shell-bridge work.</li>
<li>DotScript scripts and smoke/canary workflows.</li>
<li><code>BUILDLMDB</code>, <code>REINDEX</code>, and index lifecycle work.</li>
<li>workspace open/save/load flows.</li>
<li><code>CMDHELPCHK</code> validation.</li>
<li>maintenance scripts under <code>dottalkpp/scripts</code> and related generated proposal packages.</li>
</ul>
<p>Use this name for the public front-end/workbench family and its repeatable
workflow surfaces, not as a claim that a separate complete product exists.</p>
