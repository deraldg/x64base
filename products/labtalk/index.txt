9:T4ac,<h2 id="overview"><a class="no-underline" href="#overview">Overview</a></h2>
<p>LabTalk is the education and lab-facing framing for DotTalk++ / x64base.</p>
<p>The local project supports this direction through:</p>
<ul>
<li>visible table, record, field, index, relation, and workspace concepts.</li>
<li>command-centered learning through DotTalk++.</li>
<li>TUI, wxWidgets, and Python/Tk GUI lanes for command discovery and teaching.</li>
<li>docs and manuals focused on database literacy.</li>
<li>sample and smoke workflows such as MCC-style relation browsing and metadata workspace exercises.</li>
</ul>
<p>LabTalk should be described as an educational/lab program direction, not as a finished licensed distribution unless that packaging is explicitly created.</p>
