# Code of Conduct

Project discussions should be respectful, specific, and evidence-led.
Disagreement about architecture, compatibility, historical interpretation, or
project scope is welcome when it addresses the work rather than the person.

Harassment, threats, discriminatory conduct, deliberate disclosure of private
information, and repeated bad-faith disruption are not accepted. Report a
conduct concern privately through the contact route at
<https://x64base.com/contact/>.

The maintainer may remove material or restrict participation when necessary to
protect contributors and the project.
